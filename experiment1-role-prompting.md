# Experiment 1: Role Prompting

## Objective
Test whether assigning a role to the AI improves explanation quality.

## Base Prompt
Explain blockchain.

## Role Prompt
You are a university professor teaching computer science.
Explain blockchain in simple terms with a real-life example.

## Observation
The role prompt produced a more structured and educational explanation.

## Conclusion
Role prompting improves clarity and depth of responses.
